Why this site exists

About Memory Magnets

Memory Magnets is an independent educational website about personalized photo magnets, the stories they preserve and the practical details that make them work.

Our focusPurposeApproachIndependence

Our purpose

Thousands of photos live on phones and rarely return to view. A memory magnet offers a simple alternative: one carefully chosen image placed inside an everyday routine. This site helps readers understand the format, choose suitable photos, compare making methods and create more thoughtful gifts.

Our approach

Each guide addresses a specific reader question. We favor direct explanations, realistic tradeoffs, accessible language and original illustrations. We do not add facts simply to make a page longer, repeat keywords to manipulate search rankings or publish large numbers of thin pages aimed at minor phrase variations.

Dates appear on articles so readers can see when guidance was prepared or materially reviewed. Process instructions include safety and material cautions where they matter. If commercial recommendations are added later, they will be labeled clearly and evaluated using published criteria.

Independent, not affiliated

Memory Magnets is not affiliated with Memory Magnets Co. or another similarly named seller. Brand names belong to their respective owners. The current site is informational and does not sell magnet-making machines, photo magnets or production supplies.
